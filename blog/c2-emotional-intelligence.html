Emotional Intelligence

Search Inside Of Yourself

I took from the chapter, 'What Emotional Intelligence Is and How To Develop It', that the traditional notion of intelligence and how we perceive intelligence is skewed.
The ability to work as a team and to able to read the signs our peers give off is far more valuable to an organisation than one brain with all the brawn.
I liked the part about our brains manifesting and intensifying our emotions, and that listening to our bodies is a more sound idea.
From my two minutes of mindfulness, I forgot about the stresses of study and work and found time to relax while focusing on something simple like my breathing.
