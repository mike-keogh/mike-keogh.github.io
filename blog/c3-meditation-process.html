Meditation

The process over product concept made it very hard for me to build my site! There is still much I want to complete but could not if I wanted to stick to the concept. It is good to let go though, and appreciate the point of the exercise. I am aware that I can be a perfectionist so it is good to take a step back and focus on the process.

Before I read chapter two, I had sometimes tried meditation, and knew little about its history and real purpose. I have learnt that meditation is exercise for the mind. Given that meditation is all about attention, it makes sense that it is used to train the brain into attention.

I think that mindfulness meditation is something I will try and implement into my routine. Having felt the stress associated with programming it would be nice to practise new techniques for dealing with stress, and also for keeping concentration for longer.
