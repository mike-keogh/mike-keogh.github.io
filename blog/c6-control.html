Control

When programming, I have found that my biggest triggers are usually when I cannot get something to work, or I am not understanding some concept or idea. This week, like most of the others had a lot of triggers for me and since reading the Search Inside yourself material I have found it a lot easier to deal with these triggers. The Siberian North Railroad exercise is very similar to the other responses I have been trying to develop when dealing with said triggers. I need to remove myself from my computer sometimes to help fully use the methods and reduce the emotional response. I find breathing and noticing the problem to be the biggest helps. Sometimes it is not possible to solve a given problem right away so it helps to remember these steps during the hard times.
